控制台抽奖小程序，基于C/C++实现。
会员身份分为黄金会员、白银会员、普通会员三档，每档有着不同的中奖率。
奖品分为一等奖、二等奖和三等奖。
适合C/C++的初学者学习。

Lucky draw program on console, based on C/C++ implementation.
Membership is divided into three levels: gold member, silver member and ordinary member. Each level has different winning rate.
Prizes are divided into first prize, second prize and third prize.
Suitable for C/C++ beginners.
